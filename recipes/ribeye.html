Ribeye

Description!

This amazing cut of beef is definitely a favorite and in my opinion, one of the most flavorful out there. There is no need for special or fancy ingredients, just steaks, seasonings, a brush of oil, and a pat of butter for perfection.

Ingredients!

  • 2 ribeye steaks
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • steak seasoning
  • 2 tablespoons butter

Steps!

  1. Remove steaks from the fridge at least 45 minutes before cooking.
  2. Just before cooking, rub steaks with olive oil and generously season to taste.
  3. Preheat grill to medium heat (approximately 375Â°F).
  4. Prepare steaks as above and grill 5-6 minutes per side for medium-rare or 6-7 minutes per side for medium.
  5. Remove from the grill and top with a pat of butter, loosely tent with foil. Rest steaks 5-10 minutes before serving.
